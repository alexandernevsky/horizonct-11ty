Physical commodity trading requires seamless coordination across multiple transport modes. Our integrated rail-to-sea logistics approach has achieved a 98% on-time delivery rate, ensuring buyers receive their cargo exactly when needed.

## The Logistics Challenge

Commodity supply chains involve multiple critical stages:

### 1. Origin Logistics
- **Mine-to-Rail**: Coordinating rail wagon allocation
- **Loading Operations**: Efficient loading to minimize demurrage
- **Documentation**: Export permits and customs clearance

### 2. Rail Transport
- **Wagon Management**: Ensuring adequate rolling stock
- **Route Optimization**: Selecting most efficient rail corridors
- **Transit Time**: Managing schedules to meet vessel windows

### 3. Port Operations
- **Terminal Handling**: Efficient port operations
- **Storage Management**: Proper storage to maintain quality
- **Vessel Coordination**: Matching cargo readiness with vessel schedules

### 4. Sea Transport
- **Vessel Chartering**: Selecting appropriate vessels
- **Loading Operations**: Efficient port loading
- **Ocean Transit**: Managing voyage schedules
- **Discharge Planning**: Coordinating discharge port operations
