Quality control is fundamental to reliable commodity trading. At Horizon Commodities Trading, we mandate independent verification by ISO/IEC 17025 accredited laboratories for every shipment, ensuring 100% compliance with contracted specifications.

## ISO 17025 Standard

ISO/IEC 17025 is the international standard for testing and calibration laboratories. Accreditation under this standard ensures:

- **Technical Competence**: Laboratories demonstrate capability to perform specific tests
- **Quality Management**: Systematic approach to quality assurance
- **International Recognition**: Results accepted globally
- **Continuous Improvement**: Regular audits and proficiency testing

## Our Quality Assurance Process

### 1. Pre-Shipment Testing
Before any cargo leaves the origin, we arrange independent sampling and analysis:

- **Representative Sampling**: Certified samplers ensure representative samples
- **Multiple Parameters**: Testing covers all specification requirements
- **Documentation**: Complete test certificates accompany each shipment

### 2. Load Port Verification
At the loading port, we verify:

- **Specification Compliance**: All parameters within contracted ranges
- **Consistency**: Quality matches pre-shipment test results
- **Documentation**: Complete load port certificates

### 3. Discharge Port Confirmation
Upon arrival, final verification ensures:

- **Quality Preservation**: No degradation during transport
- **Specification Match**: Confirms contracted quality delivered
- **Dispute Resolution**: Objective third-party verification
