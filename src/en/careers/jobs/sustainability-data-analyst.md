## Job Description

We are seeking a Sustainability Data Analyst to track, analyze, and report on environmental metrics across our supply chain operations. This role involves collecting data, performing analysis, and preparing reports for stakeholders.

## Key Responsibilities

- Collect and analyze sustainability data from supply chain operations
- Prepare reports on environmental metrics and impact
- Identify trends and opportunities for improvement
- Work with operations team to implement data collection processes
- Ensure data accuracy and quality
- Present findings to management and stakeholders

## Requirements

- Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science, Data Science, or related field
- 2+ years of experience in data analysis or sustainability reporting
- Strong analytical and statistical skills
- Proficiency in Excel, SQL, and data visualization tools
- Knowledge of sustainability frameworks and metrics
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
