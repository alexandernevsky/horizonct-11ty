## Job Description

We are seeking an experienced Supply Chain Analyst to manage and optimize our coal and bulk commodities supply chain operations. This role involves coordinating logistics from minehead to discharge port, ensuring quality compliance, and maintaining relationships with producers and logistics partners.

## Key Responsibilities

- Analyze and optimize supply chain operations for coal and bulk commodities
- Coordinate rail, port, and vessel logistics
- Monitor quality compliance and coordinate independent inspections
- Manage relationships with producers and logistics partners
- Track shipments and ensure on-time delivery
- Prepare reports and analysis for management

## Requirements

- Bachelor's degree in Supply Chain Management, Logistics, or related field
- 3+ years of experience in physical commodity trading or logistics
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent communication and negotiation abilities
- Knowledge of Incoterms® 2020 and international trade practices
- Proficiency in Excel and data analysis tools
