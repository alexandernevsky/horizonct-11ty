## Job Description

We are seeking a Supply Chain Consultant to provide strategic guidance and optimize supply chain operations for our clients. This role involves analyzing supply chains, identifying improvements, and implementing solutions.

## Key Responsibilities

- Analyze client supply chains and identify optimization opportunities
- Develop and implement supply chain strategies
- Provide consulting services to industrial clients
- Prepare reports and recommendations
- Work with cross-functional teams to implement solutions
- Stay current with industry trends and best practices

## Requirements

- Master's degree in Supply Chain Management, Business, or related field
- 7+ years of experience in supply chain consulting or management
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent presentation and communication abilities
- Experience in physical commodity trading preferred
- Ability to travel as needed
