## Job Description

We are looking for a Customer Success Manager to build and maintain strong relationships with our industrial clients. This role focuses on ensuring customer satisfaction, managing accounts, and identifying opportunities for growth.

## Key Responsibilities

- Build and maintain relationships with key industrial clients
- Ensure customer satisfaction and address any concerns
- Coordinate with internal teams to deliver on customer requirements
- Identify opportunities for account growth and upselling
- Prepare regular reports on account status and customer feedback
- Serve as the primary point of contact for assigned accounts

## Requirements

- Bachelor's degree in Business, Engineering, or related field
- 5+ years of experience in account management or customer success
- Experience in B2B industrial sales or trading preferred
- Strong interpersonal and communication skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced, dynamic environment
- Fluency in English; additional languages a plus
