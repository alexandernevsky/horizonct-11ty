About Us

Learn about Horizon Commodities Trading LTD and our mission to transform global commodity trading

Horizon Commodities Trading LTD

Horizon Commodities Trading LTD is a Hong Kong registered company specializing in physical commodity trading with a focus on energy, metallurgy, and construction materials.

Our Mission

We transform the complexity of global physical trading into a streamlined, strategic advantage, ensuring your operations run on verified quality and predictable schedules.

Our Approach

We source directly from vetted producers and suppliers, ensuring origin transparency, spec alignment, and structured contracting under Incoterms® 2020 standards.

Quality Assurance

Every shipment is verified by independent ISO/IEC 17025 accredited laboratories (SGS/Intertek) prior to dispatch, ensuring 100% quality compliance.

Global Logistics

Our team coordinates the entire supply chain — from rail wagon allocation at the mine to port terminal handling and vessel chartering — ensuring your cargo arrives on schedule with a 98% on-time delivery rate.
