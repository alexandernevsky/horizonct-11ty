## About Horizon Commodities Trading Limited

In the complex world of physical commodities trading, efficiency and reliability are paramount. Horizon Commodities Trading Limited is a Hong Kong-based company specializing in the sourcing and delivery of thermal and metallurgical coal to industrial end-users across Asia. We don't just trade paper; we manage the physical journey of essential resources from mine to market, ensuring energy security for power generation and steel production.

## The Role

As a Customer Success Manager, you will be the primary point of contact for our industrial clients, ensuring their needs are met and expectations exceeded. You will work closely with our operations and trading teams to coordinate seamless delivery of coal shipments, manage client relationships, and identify opportunities for growth.

## What We Are Looking For

We need someone who understands the critical importance of reliable supply chains for industrial operations. You will:

* ### Client Relationship Management Build and maintain strong relationships with key industrial clients, understanding their operational requirements and ensuring our services align with their needs.
* ### Supply Chain Coordination Work with internal teams to coordinate shipments, manage logistics, and ensure timely delivery of coal to client facilities.
* ### Problem Resolution Act as the first point of contact for any issues or concerns, working proactively to resolve problems before they impact client operations.
* ### Account Growth Identify opportunities to expand our services with existing clients and support business development efforts.

## The Right Candidate Will Have

* ### Strong Communication Skills Ability to communicate effectively with clients, internal teams, and stakeholders at all levels.
* ### Problem-Solving Ability Capability to quickly identify issues and develop solutions that maintain service quality and client satisfaction.
* ### Industry Knowledge Understanding of coal markets, industrial operations, and supply chain logistics.
* ### Customer-Focused Mindset Dedication to ensuring client success and building long-term partnerships.

## Working at Horizon

At Horizon, we believe that strong client relationships are the foundation of our business. We are committed to providing exceptional service and building partnerships that last. By joining our team, you will play a crucial role in ensuring our clients receive the reliable supply they need to power their operations.
