**About Horizon Commodities Trading Limited**

In the complex world of physical commodities trading, efficiency and reliability are paramount. Horizon Commodities Trading Limited is a Hong Kong-based company specializing in the sourcing and delivery of thermal and metallurgical coal to industrial end-users across Asia. We don't just trade paper; we manage the physical journey of essential resources from mine to market, ensuring energy security for power generation and steel production.

**The Role**

We are seeking an experienced Supply Chain Consultant to help optimize our logistics operations and improve efficiency across our supply chain. This role involves analyzing our current operations, identifying improvement opportunities, and implementing solutions that enhance both cost efficiency and service reliability.

**What We Are Looking For**

You will work closely with our operations team to:

* **Process Optimization:** Analyze current supply chain processes and identify opportunities for improvement in efficiency, cost, and reliability.
* **Strategic Planning:** Develop strategies for optimizing logistics corridors, reducing transit times, and minimizing costs.
* **Performance Analysis:** Track key performance metrics and develop reporting systems to monitor supply chain performance.
* **Risk Management:** Identify potential risks in the supply chain and develop mitigation strategies.

**The Right Candidate Will Have**

* **Supply Chain Expertise:** Deep understanding of logistics operations, particularly in bulk commodity transportation.
* **Analytical Skills:** Ability to analyze complex data sets and identify trends and opportunities.
* **Project Management:** Experience managing improvement projects from conception to implementation.
* **Communication Skills:** Ability to present findings and recommendations clearly to stakeholders.

**Working at Horizon**

At Horizon, we are committed to continuous improvement and operational excellence. By joining our team, you will have the opportunity to make a real impact on our operations and help shape the future of our supply chain.

**Join Us**

If you have a passion for supply chain optimization and want to work with a team that values innovation and continuous improvement, we want to hear from you.
