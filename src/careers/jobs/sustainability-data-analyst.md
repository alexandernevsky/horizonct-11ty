**About Horizon Commodities Trading Limited**

In the complex world of physical commodities trading, efficiency and reliability are paramount. Horizon Commodities Trading Limited is a Hong Kong-based company specializing in the sourcing and delivery of thermal and metallurgical coal to industrial end-users across Asia. We don't just trade paper; we manage the physical journey of essential resources from mine to market, ensuring energy security for power generation and steel production.

**The Role**

We are seeking a Sustainability Data Analyst to help track, analyze, and improve our environmental performance. This role involves collecting environmental data, analyzing trends, and providing insights that help us reduce our environmental impact while maintaining operational efficiency.

**What We Are Looking For**

You will be responsible for:

* **Data Collection:** Gather environmental data from various sources across our supply chain, including emissions, energy consumption, and waste metrics.
* **Data Analysis:** Analyze environmental data to identify trends, patterns, and opportunities for improvement.
* **Reporting:** Develop and maintain sustainability reports that provide clear visibility into our environmental performance.
* **Recommendations:** Provide actionable recommendations for reducing environmental impact based on data analysis.

**The Right Candidate Will Have**

* **Data Analysis Skills:** Strong analytical skills and experience working with data analysis tools.
* **Sustainability Knowledge:** Understanding of environmental metrics, carbon accounting, and sustainability reporting frameworks.
* **Attention to Detail:** Ability to ensure data accuracy and identify inconsistencies.
* **Communication Skills:** Capability to present findings and recommendations clearly to stakeholders.

**Working at Horizon**

At Horizon, we are committed to reducing our environmental impact while maintaining the reliability our clients depend on. By joining our team, you will play a crucial role in helping us achieve our sustainability goals.

**Join Us**

If you are passionate about sustainability and want to use data to drive positive environmental change, we want to hear from you.
