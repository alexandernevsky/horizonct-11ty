**About Horizon Commodities Trading Limited**

In the complex world of physical commodities trading, efficiency and reliability are paramount. Horizon Commodities Trading Limited is a Hong Kong-based company specializing in the sourcing and delivery of thermal and metallurgical coal to industrial end-users across Asia. We don't just trade paper; we manage the physical journey of essential resources from mine to market, ensuring energy security for power generation and steel production.

**The Role**

We are seeking a Software Engineer to develop sustainability solutions that help optimize our operations and reduce environmental impact. This role involves building software tools and platforms that enable us to track, analyze, and improve our environmental performance while maintaining operational efficiency.

**What We Are Looking For**

You will work on developing:

* **Sustainability Tracking Systems:** Build platforms to track carbon emissions, energy consumption, and other environmental metrics across our supply chain.
* **Data Analytics Tools:** Develop tools for analyzing environmental data and identifying opportunities for improvement.
* **Reporting Dashboards:** Create dashboards that provide clear visibility into our sustainability performance.
* **Integration Solutions:** Integrate sustainability tracking with our existing operational systems.

**The Right Candidate Will Have**

* **Software Development Skills:** Strong programming skills in modern languages and frameworks.
* **Data Analytics Experience:** Experience working with data analytics tools and platforms.
* **Sustainability Knowledge:** Understanding of environmental metrics and sustainability reporting.
* **Problem-Solving Ability:** Capability to translate business requirements into technical solutions.

**Working at Horizon**

At Horizon, we believe that technology can be a powerful force for positive change. By developing sustainability solutions, you will help us reduce our environmental impact while maintaining the reliability our clients depend on.

**Join Us**

If you are passionate about using technology to drive sustainability and want to work on meaningful projects that make a real difference, we want to hear from you.
